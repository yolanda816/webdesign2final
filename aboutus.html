Our Story

DS cake is the Best Spouge Cake Bakery in Fairfax, VA. We use the finest ingredients in our cakes and cookies which gives you premium quality and taste. Each cake is made entirely from scratch using fresh, all natural ingredients. The simple ingredients of butter, sugar, flour, eggs, and cream turn into the most glorious cake layers and fillings. Cakes are the centerpieces of any special event, and DS Cake stands out with clean designs, modern silhouettes, and delicious taste.

We currently service in the cities of Fairfax, VA. We are ready with a fleet of vehicles, to provide you with fast and continuous service, seven days a week, including holidays. To place an order or if you have any questions, please send us an email, and our team will respond as soon as possible.


IMPORTANT

Some cakes may include nuts and/or other allergens. No guarantee can be made to people with allergies, if you do have an allergy, please let me know beforehand so I can make sure that your order hasn’t touched any nuts/ allergens.
Vegan, Egg Free, Dairy Free or Gluten Free cookies are available upon request. Please email or call us at least 2 Weeks advance for all requests.
